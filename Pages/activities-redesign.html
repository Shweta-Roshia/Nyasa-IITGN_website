Celebrating our memorable past events that made a difference

Ganesh Chaturthi Celebration

We celebrated the auspicious festival of Ganesh Chaturthi with our children and community members. The event included cultural performances, traditional activities, and created a joyous atmosphere for all participants.

Cultural Event

Wildlife Week Celebration

Our wildlife week celebration aimed to raise awareness about environmental conservation and the importance of protecting our natural habitats. Children participated in educational activities and outdoor programs.

Environmental

Taare Zameen Par Screening

A special screening of the inspirational film "Taare Zameen Par" was organized for our children. The movie sparked meaningful discussions about learning differences and the importance of understanding and support.

Educational

Our specialized teaching sessions cover topics beyond regular curriculum. Expert volunteers conduct interactive sessions on subjects ranging from science, arts, computer skills to personality development.

Sports & Recreation

Regular sports sessions are conducted to keep the children active and healthy. These activities include football, cricket, games, and outdoor play that promote physical fitness and team spirit.
